## The Problem

1. Manual data entry from PDF invoices wastes 2–6 hours/month per person
2. Missed invoices and receipts lead to lost tax deductions
3. Bookkeeping tools like Dext/Hubdoc cost $50–$300/month
4. Remote teams have no centralized, automatic way to track vendor expenses
5. Accountants bill hourly for work that AI can do in seconds

## The Solution

A Gmail-triggered workflow that:
- Watches for emails with invoice/receipt/bill keywords and downloads attachments
- Extracts PDF text and sends it to GPT-4o-mini for structured data extraction
- Pulls 10+ fields: vendor, amount, tax, date, due date, category, payment status
- Appends a clean, formatted row to Google Sheets automatically
- Optionally pings Slack with a summary notification

## How It Works

Node-by-node flow:

- **Gmail Trigger** — Polls for unread invoice emails with attachments
- **IF** — Guards against emails with no attachments
- **Extract From File** — Pulls raw text from PDF attachments
- **OpenAI (gpt-4o-mini)** — Structured JSON extraction from document text
- **Code** — Safely parses AI response, handles edge cases
- **Google Sheets** — Appends 15 columns of financial data per row
- **Slack** — Team notification (optional)
- **No-Op** — Silent skip for emails without attachments
