## The Problem

1. Generic "Hi {{first_name}}, welcome!" emails are ignored — newsletter open rates crater after email #1
2. Manual personalized welcome DMs don't scale beyond 10–20 new subscribers per day
3. Platforms like ConvertKit and Beehiiv charge $79–$299/month but still send template-based welcomes
4. No intelligence on subscriber quality — founders can't tell a potential $5k client from a one-time reader
5. High-value business subscribers sign up and get the same email as everyone else — missed sales opportunity

## The Solution

A webhook-triggered n8n workflow that:
- Receives new subscriber data from any newsletter platform (ConvertKit, Beehiiv, Mailchimp, custom forms)
- Sends name, email, source, tags, and interests to GPT-4o-mini for hyper-personalized welcome email generation
- Detects business vs. personal email domain automatically and scores subscriber quality (1–10 VIP score)
- Routes VIP subscribers to a priority email path AND fires a Slack alert with their full profile for manual follow-up
- Logs all 15 subscriber intelligence fields to Google Sheets, building a subscriber quality database over time

## How It Works

Node-by-node flow:

- **Webhook** — Receives new subscriber POST from newsletter platform
- **Set** — Extracts and normalizes subscriber fields (name, email, source, tags, interests)
- **IF** — Guards against missing email → 400 error
- **OpenAI (gpt-4o-mini)** — Generates unique subject line + email body + VIP score as JSON
- **Code** — Parses AI response, detects business email domain, calculates final VIP status
- **IF** — Routes VIP vs. standard subscriber
- **Gmail (×2)** — Sends VIP or standard welcome email
- **Google Sheets** — Logs 15-column subscriber intelligence record
- **IF** — Routes VIP subs to Slack alert
- **Slack** — VIP lead alert with full subscriber profile and personalization details
- **No Op** — Silent pass for standard subs
- **Respond to Webhook (×2)** — Returns success JSON or 400 error
