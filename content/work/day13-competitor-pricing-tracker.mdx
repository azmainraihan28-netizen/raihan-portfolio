## The Problem

1. Pricing changes happen overnight — founders find out weeks later when they lose deals
2. Manual monitoring of 5 competitor pages wastes 30–45 minutes of daily ops time
3. Sales reps quote prices without knowing if a competitor just slashed theirs by 20%
4. Paid tools (Crayon, Klue, Kompyte) cost $500–$2,000/month just for pricing intelligence
5. No historical record — without a tracker, you can't spot trends, seasonal discounts, or pricing experiments

## The Solution

A scheduled weekday workflow that:
- Scrapes configured competitor pricing pages every morning at 8 AM via HTTP Request
- Strips HTML noise and passes a clean text snippet to GPT-4o-mini
- AI extracts 13 structured fields: price, annual rate, model, free tier, trial, discounts, features, limits, summary
- Logs a 17-column row to Google Sheets — building a historical pricing intelligence database
- Sends a formatted Slack morning digest with all competitor data and strategic one-liners
- Cost: ~$0.001 per competitor per day

## How It Works

Node-by-node flow:

- **Schedule Trigger** — Mon–Fri 8 AM
- **Set** — Config block: competitor URLs, Sheets ID, Slack toggle, alert threshold
- **Split Out** — Iterates one competitor at a time
- **HTTP Request** — Scrapes raw pricing page HTML
- **Code** — Strips HTML, extracts clean text snippet (3,000 chars)
- **OpenAI (gpt-4o-mini)** — Extracts 13 structured pricing fields as JSON
- **Code** — Parses AI response, adds date/time metadata
- **Google Sheets** — Appends 17-column log row
- **IF** — Checks if Slack enabled
- **Aggregate** — Collects all competitors before digest
- **Code** — Builds formatted Slack digest message
- **Slack** — Posts morning pricing intelligence digest
- **No Op** — Silent pass when Slack is disabled
