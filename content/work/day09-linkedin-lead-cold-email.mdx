## The Problem

1. Generic cold emails get under 1% response rates — prospects ignore template blasts instantly
2. Manual personalization doesn't scale — writing custom emails for 50 leads takes 3–5 hours
3. Hiring SDRs costs $3,000–$5,000/month for work AI completes in 90 seconds
4. No logging or visibility — teams lose track of who was contacted and with what message
5. Copy-paste errors (wrong name, wrong company) destroy credibility and deals

## The Solution

A webhook-triggered workflow that:
- Accepts a batch of lead objects (name, role, company, bio, pain context)
- Uses GPT-4o-mini to generate a unique subject line + hyper-personalized email body per lead
- Routes to Gmail: either auto-sends (mode: "send") or saves as draft (mode: "draft")
- Logs every email to Google Sheets with subject, body, icebreaker, and open rate estimate
- Notifies Slack when the full batch is processed

## How It Works

Node-by-node flow:

- **Webhook** — Receives lead batch (POST)
- **Set** — Extracts sender config and mode
- **IF** — Guards against empty lead arrays
- **Split Out** — Iterates over each lead individually
- **OpenAI (gpt-4o-mini)** — Generates personalized subject + email body as JSON
- **Code** — Safely parses AI output, fallback template on error
- **Google Sheets** — Logs all 12 fields per lead
- **IF** — Routes to Send vs. Draft based on mode flag
- **Gmail (×2)** — Sends email OR saves as draft
- **Aggregate** — Collects all results before notification
- **Slack** — Batch completion alert
- **Respond to Webhook** — Returns success JSON with count
