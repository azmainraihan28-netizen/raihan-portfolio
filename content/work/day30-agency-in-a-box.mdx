## The Problem

1. Manual lead follow-up kills 80% of potential deals — reps forget to follow up after the first email
2. Writing personalized proposals from scratch for every prospect wastes 2–4 hours per deal
3. Client onboarding is inconsistent — new clients get different experiences depending on who handles them
4. No unified system to track the full pipeline: leads → proposals → clients → deliverables
5. Agencies spend 30–50% of their time on ops tasks (emails, logging, Slack updates) instead of billable work

## The Solution

A single n8n webhook-triggered workflow with three smart branches controlled by a `stage` field:
- **`new_lead`** → AI qualifies lead (score 1–10, ICP match, is_hot flag) → sends personalized pitch email → Day 3 + Day 7 follow-ups via Wait nodes → logs to Google Sheets → Slack hot lead alert
- **`proposal`** → AI generates full HTML proposal (deliverables, timeline, investment, ROI) → sends email → logs to Sheets → Slack alert
- **`onboard`** → AI writes personalized onboarding package (welcome + Day 3 check-in + Day 7 milestone emails) → sends all 3 with Wait nodes → logs client to Sheets → team Slack announcement

## How It Works

Node-by-node flow:

- (33 total)
- **Webhook** — Single entry point for all 3 stages (POST /agency-in-a-box)
- **Set** — Normalizes 15 input fields with safe defaults
- **IF (Validate)** — Guards against missing lead_name/email → 400 error
- **Switch** — Routes to new_lead / proposal / onboard branch
- **HTTP Request (OpenAI ×3)** — GPT-4o-mini: lead qualifier, proposal writer, onboarding generator (JSON mode)
- **Code (×3)** — Safely parses each AI JSON response with full fallback templates
- **Gmail (×7)** — Pitch email, Day 3 follow-up, Day 7 follow-up, proposal, welcome, Day 3 client check-in, Day 7 milestone
- **Wait (×4)** — 3-day and 4-day delays (both lead follow-up and client check-in sequences)
- **Google Sheets (×3)** — Logs leads, proposals, and clients to separate sheets
- **Slack (×3)** — Hot lead alert, proposal sent alert, new client announcement
- **IF (hot lead check)** — Routes hot leads to Slack, others to NoOp
- **NoOp** — Silent pass for warm/cold leads
- **Respond to Webhook (×2)** — Returns 200 success JSON or 400 validation error
